RiseUp is responding to flood-affected Assam

Previous Next
Heavy rainfall and flood continue to wreak havoc in Guwahati, Assam. The water level of river Brahmaputra has been rising due to incessant rains. Flood has claimed lives of at least 11 people in Assam and has threatened the lives of approximately 26.5 lakhs people in the state. And it is worsening every hour!
About

RiseUp Foundation has stepped in to provide food to affected people in Assam from its kitchen in Guwahati.
The Foundation will provide meals twice a day to approximately 700 people of Athgaon region in Kamrup district.

Precautions

Stay informed about flooding conditions in your area.
Don't try to walk through a flooded area.
Avoid flooded areas if you are in a vehicle.
A stalled car should be abandoned immediately.
Evacuate a flooded area immediately if instructed to do so.
