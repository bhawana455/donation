Precautions

Stay informed about flooding conditions in your area.
Don't try to walk through a flooded area.
Avoid flooded areas if you are in a vehicle.
A stalled car should be abandoned immediately.
Evacuate a flooded area immediately if instructed to do so.
Do not touch electrical equipment if you are wet or standing in water
Avoid low spots, like ditches, basements, or underpasses and Stay out of any building if it is surrounded by floodwaters.
